Safe Web Practices

Protecting your personal information online is crucial. Check out these resources to learn more about web safety:

  • CISA: Secure Our World - CISA provides actionable steps like using strong passwords and enabling MFA to protect your online identity.
  • FTC: Online Security - The FTC offers consumer advice on how to secure your devices and protect personal information online.
  • National Cybersecurity Alliance - This organization educates users on identifying phishing attempts and maintaining basic digital hygiene.
