Size-Based Pricing Guide

Grooming costs vary depending on the size of your pet, as larger pets require more time and product.

  • Small (0 - 20 lbs): Base Price
  • Medium (21 - 50 lbs): Base Price + $10
  • Large (51 - 90 lbs): Base Price + $20
  • Extra Large (91+ lbs): Base Price + $35

*Note: Exact prices are finalized after evaluating coat condition.
